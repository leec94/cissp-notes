# Overview 
## Required Experience 
Facts about the CISSP  
- Must have 5 years cumulative full-time work experience in 2 or more of the 8 domains
  - 4 years if you have a college degree or approved certification 
  - years of experience includes part-time work and internship 
  - if not enough experience, can become an Associate of ISC2

## Exam Format 
- Format is CAT, adaptive testing, with 100 - 150 questions 
- Passing scores are at least 700 out of 1000 points 
- 25 unscored questions 
- Max 3 hours to take exam
- Cannot change answer or go back
- Can retake the exam under certain conditions 
- Most questions are 4 option multiple choice questions, some advanced question formats like drag-and-drop and hotspot questions 
- After the exam, you must get an endorsement from someone else with an active ISC2 cert within 9 months 
- To maintain certification, must get 120 Continuing Education Credits (CPE) by 3rd year, and pay annual fee
