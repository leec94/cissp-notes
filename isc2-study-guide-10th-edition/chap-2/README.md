# Chapter 2: Personnel Security and Risk Management Concepts
## Notes
- Hiring, onboarding, offboarding processes
- Principle of least privilege: users should be granted minimum amount of access necessary to complete work tasks or job responsibilities 
- Employee oversight: reviewing and auditing of job descriptions, work tasks, privileges, and responsibilities 
- Mandatory vacations: used to audit and verify the work tasks and privileges of employees, to detect abuse, fraud, negligence 
- user behavior analytics (UBA) or user and entity behavior analytics (UEBA): analyzing behavior or users, subjects, visitors, customers and more for a specific goal or purpose 
- employee transfer processes: depends on organization 
- termination policy: procedure for terminating employees
  - always have a witness 
  - disable employee's network access 
  - perform exit interview
  - escort terminated employee off premises 
  - return of security tokens, badges, company property
- risk analysis: process where upper management is provided with details to made decisions about which risks are to be mitigated, transferred, and accepted 
- evaluate threats: can be from sources like IT, humans, and nature. should be performed as a team effort for the widest range of perspectives 
- qualitative risk analysis 
- quantitative risk analysis 
- single loss expectancy (SLE): asset value (AV) * exposure factor (EF)
- annualized loss expectancy (ALE): SLE * annualized rate of occurrence (ARO)
